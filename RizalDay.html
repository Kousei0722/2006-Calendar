Regular Holiday

Holiday Name Rizal Day
What Do People Do? On Rizal Day, December 30, people in the Philippines observe the death anniversary of their national hero, Dr. Jos√© Rizal, through solemn ceremonies like wreath-laying and flag-raising at Rizal Park in Manila. Schools and local governments host their own events, often featuring performances and readings of Rizal's works to honor his patriotism and vision for the nation. As a national non-working holiday, many also use the day off to visit Rizal-related sites or reflect on his legacy and contributions to Filipino identity and the struggle for independence.

Rizal Day is a public holiday. It is a day off for the general population, and schools and most businesses are closed.
