Regular Holiday

Holiday Name Christmas Day
What Do People Do? On Christmas Day, people typically exchange gifts, enjoy a special meal with family and friends, listen to Christmas music, and watch holiday movies. Many attend church services, while others participate in secular activities such as visiting festive light displays, volunteering, or engaging in outdoor activities. The day's traditions blend Christian and secular customs, with a focus on togetherness and celebration.

Christmas Day, on December 25, is one of the most festive Christian holidays in many countries around the world. It celebrates Jesus' birth.
