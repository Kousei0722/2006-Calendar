Regular Holiday

Holiday Name National Heroes Day
What Do People Do? On National Heroes Day in the Philippines, people commemorate the sacrifices of heroes for the nation's freedom and independence through wreath-laying ceremonies, parades, and official festivities at various shrines and monuments. It's also a time for Filipinos to reflect on their shared history and values, such as community spirit (bayanihan) and unity (pagkakaisa), and to honor the contributions of both known and unknown heroes.

National Heroes Day is a public holiday. It is a day off for the general population, and schools and most businesses are closed.
