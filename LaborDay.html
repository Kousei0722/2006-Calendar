Regular Holiday

Holiday Name Labor Day
What Do People Do? On Labor Day, people celebrate by participating in community events like parades, enjoying outdoor activities such as picnics, barbecues, and swimming, and taking a long weekend to relax with family and friends. The holiday, observed on the first Monday of September in North America, honors workers and symbolizes the end of summer. In some countries, like the Philippines, Labor Day also includes demonstrations and rallies to highlight worker issues and advocate for their rights.

Labor Day is also known as International Workers’ Day and May Day. It’s a public holiday in many countries worldwide. It usually occurs around May 1, but several countries observe it on other dates.
